Understanding how mind emerges from matter is one of the great remaining questions in science. The Artificial Cognitive Systems lab, headed by <a href='https://www.ru.nl/personen/gerven-m-van/' className='lightBlue'>Marcel van Gerven</a>,
studies the computational principles that underlie natural intelligence and uses these principles to develop more capable and efficient intelligent technology. You can check out our publications <a href='https://scholar.google.nl/citations?user=sX0ZypwAAAAJ&hl=nl' className='lightBlue'>here</a>.
